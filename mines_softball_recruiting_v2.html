A Proven Builder of Champions

Colorado Native  ·  Former D1 All-American  ·  13 Seasons as Collegiate Head Coach

Coach Echo-Hawk brings an extraordinary wealth of Division I coaching experience to Golden. A Louisville, Colorado native, she returns home after head coaching stints at Portland State, UTEP, and staff positions at New Mexico State — where she won a WAC Championship.

As a player at Nebraska, she was a two-time All-American and still ranks among the all-time Husker leaders in batting average (2nd, .379), hits (1st, 266), and runs scored (4th, 168).

Life at Mines

Golden, Colorado is Home

20 minutes from Denver, in the foothills of the Rockies, Mines gives you world-class academics, a tight-knit campus community, and one of the most spectacular places to live and compete in America.

⚡

E-Days

Engineering Days — Mines' legendary annual celebration. Cardboard boat races on Clear Creek, live music, competitions, and a campus-wide festival that captures everything that makes Mines special.

⛰

The M Climb

Every freshman hauls a 50-lb rock up Mount Zion to paint the iconic "M" above Golden. A rite of passage that defines what it means to be an Oredigger — every athlete does it too.

💼

Career Day

One of the most competitive career fairs in the nation. 1,290+ employers, 1,552+ job postings. Companies compete to hire Mines graduates — not the other way around.

🎿

The Outdoors

World-class skiing one hour away. Clear Creek for kayaking steps from campus. Hiking, climbing, mountain biking — if you love the mountains, there is no better place to be an athlete and a student.
